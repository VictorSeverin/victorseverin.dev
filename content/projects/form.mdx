Collaborated in a team to develop a versatile and user-friendly form creation application, akin to Google Forms, enabling users to effortlessly construct visually appealing forms for surveys and questionnaires; the project incorporates features allowing the collection and viewing of responses, as well as saving them in a JSON format, thereby merging aesthetic appeal with practical utility for efficient data collection and analysis.
